About InnovateTrack

InnovateTrack is a portal designed to help educational institutes measure and track their innovation excellence. By collecting data on patents, research papers, and collaborations, we aim to provide comprehensive insights into the innovative progress across departments.

Features

Innovation Tracking

Track and analyze key innovation indicators like patents, publications, and research grants.

User Role Management

Secure login with role-based access for administrators, faculty, and students.

Recognition

Recognize and reward top innovators with a built-in incentive system.
